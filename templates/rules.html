Platform Rules & Guidelines

Deposit Rules
  • Minimum deposit amount is ₹500
  • Deposits are processed within 24 hours of submission
  • Valid UTR number must be provided for all deposits
  • Deposits are subject to verification and approval
Withdrawal Rules
  • Minimum withdrawal amount is ₹1000
  • Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours
  • No withdrawals are processed on Sundays
  • Withdrawals are subject to verification and approval
  • A withdrawal fee of 5% will be deducted from the total amount at the time of withdrawal.
  • Account details must be accurate and match the registered name
Stock Investment Rules
  • Each stock has a specific package amount and tenure
  • Daily returns are credited to your wallet balance
  • Claims can be made between 6:00 AM and 10:00 PM (IST)
  • One claim per stock per day is allowed
  • Total returns are calculated based on the investment tenure
Referral Rules
  • Referral bonus of 15% of investment amount is awarded for each successful referral
  • Referred user must purchase a stock to qualify for the bonus
  • Referral bonus is subject to verification and approval
  • Referral bonus is credited to your wallet balance
General Rules
  • All transactions are subject to verification
  • Platform reserves the right to modify rules and policies
  • Users must provide accurate information during registration
  • Any fraudulent activity will result in account suspension
  • Platform is not responsible for any technical issues or delays
